# MannLabs

Open-source proteomics software developed by members of the 
[Mann Group at the University of Copenhagen](https://www.cpr.ku.dk/research/proteomics/mann/)
and the
[Mann Lab at the Max Planck Institute of Biochemistry](https://www.biochem.mpg.de/mann).

See [alphapept.org](https://www.alphapept.org) for details of our AlphaPept software ecosystem.
